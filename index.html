About Engineering Assurance

Engineering Assurance provides specialist associate consultancy support to organisations operating in safety-critical and engineering-led environments.

While our core expertise lies within the rail and light rail sectors, the principles of good engineering governance, quality management, competency assurance, reliability improvement and operational learning are applicable across many industries.

Our approach combines practical operational experience with recognised engineering, quality and management principles to deliver evidence-based, proportionate and sustainable improvements tailored to each organisation.

Services

Standards & Governance

Standards development, technical guidance, governance reviews, gap analysis, technical authoring and compliance support.

Competency Management

Competency frameworks, competence assurance, skills gap analysis, assessment systems, governance and assurance reviews.

Reliability Engineering

Pareto analysis, root cause analysis, 5 Whys, FMEA, Fault Tree Analysis, Weibull analysis, MDBF improvement and maintenance strategy reviews.

Operational Learning

Incident learning, lessons learned reviews, technical research and translating operational learning into practical improvement.

Quality Management

ISO 9001 implementation support, QMS development, process mapping, internal audit support, document control and management review preparation.

Safety & Risk

Risk assessments, engineering risk reviews, hazard identification, safety governance, technical assurance and engineering change support.

Management Development

Engineering leadership support, management development, team improvement, governance development and performance management support.
