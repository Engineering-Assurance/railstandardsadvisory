About

Engineering Assurance provides specialist associate consultancy support across rail and light rail environments, with a focus on practical, evidence-led improvement.

Areas of support include engineering governance, standards development, competency management, reliability improvement, asset performance, operational learning and safety assurance.

Services

Standards & Governance

Support with standards development, implementation, engineering governance, compliance reviews and assurance processes.

Competency Management

Competency framework development, competence assurance reviews, gap analysis, assessor support and governance arrangements.

Reliability Engineering

Reliability improvement, Pareto analysis, root cause investigation, FMEA, fault tree analysis, Weibull analysis and asset performance reviews.

Operational Learning

Lessons learned reviews, incident learning, technical research and translating operational learning into practical improvements.

Experience & Strengths

  • Over 20 years' experience across rail and light rail engineering environments.
  • Experience in operational maintenance, engineering leadership, reliability improvement and safety-critical assurance.
  • Development and implementation of competency management systems.
  • Standards and guidance development involving operators, manufacturers, regulators and industry stakeholders.
  • Participation in Railway Applications Mirror Groups.
  • Representation within selected BSI, CEN and ISO standards working groups.
  • Experience contributing to significant improvements in fleet Mean Distance Between Failures performance.
