About Engineering Assurance

Engineering Assurance provides specialist associate consultancy support to organisations operating in safety-critical and engineering-led environments.

While core expertise lies within the rail and light rail sectors, the principles of good engineering governance, quality management, competency assurance, reliability improvement and operational learning are applicable across many industries.

The approach combines practical operational experience with recognised engineering, quality and management principles to deliver evidence-based, proportionate and sustainable improvements tailored to each organisation.

Professional Approach

Every organisation faces different engineering, operational and governance challenges. Rather than offering generic solutions, Engineering Assurance works collaboratively with clients to understand the problem, identify practical opportunities for improvement and deliver proportionate, evidence-based recommendations.

  • Understand the problem and operational context
  • Review evidence, standards, processes and current controls
  • Identify practical improvement opportunities
  • Support implementation through proportionate recommendations
  • Focus on sustainable improvement rather than unnecessary complexity
